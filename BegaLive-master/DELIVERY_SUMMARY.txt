📊 MONITORING STATIONS:
=======================

The system tracks the Bega River (Serbia) at 5 locations:

1. Bega Source (Čoka)      - 46.2°N, 18.78°E   - 120m elevation
2. Bačka Palanka           - 45.78°N, 19.35°E  - 85m elevation
3. Bečej Station           - 45.92°N, 19.89°E  - 80m elevation
4. Zrenjanin Area          - 45.38°N, 20.40°E  - 75m elevation
5. Bega - Tisa Mouth       - 45.24°N, 20.16°E  - 70m elevation

📈 TRACKED METRICS:
===================

- Temperature (°C)      - Real-time with 24h trend
- pH Level              - Water acidity (6-8 range)
- Oxygen Content (mg/L) - Dissolved O₂ levels
- Water Turbidity (NTU) - Water clarity
- Flow Rate (m³/s)      - Water discharge
